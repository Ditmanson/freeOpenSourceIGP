The goal is to give power to her barks. I want her to think she can make me come to her, or make me flee based on her barks. If I ask her to get scary, we want to see powerful barks. To get this we are going to use shaping and frustration with the tug. As she barks I'll move closer. Initially, I should reward any vocal barks with forward motion and should avoid resetting. As time goes on I want to start getting stronger and more sustained barks. Eventually, this should turn into a game where I can move back or go still, and she'll bring me to life with barking.
